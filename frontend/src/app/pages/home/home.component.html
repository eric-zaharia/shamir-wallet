How it works

Save your crypto mnemonic phrase securely

This wallet uses Shamir's Secret Sharing algorithm to split your mnemonic phrase into your desired number of shares.






After your secret was split into n shares, it can be reconstructed using no less than half of them. You can choose to keep any number of the created shares, which you will receive as encrypted files to your desired email address. The rest are stored securely in our server.



Recommendations

To make sure your secret is stored as securely as possible we recommend the following:

  • Always keep a majority of the shares in your custody. This way, even if anything happens to our servers, you and only you can decrypt your secret.

  • Securely store your shares in different places (e.g. non-overlapping cloud providers, different family members, a group of trusted unrelated people, different physical locations). This way, even if you lose one share and suspect it has been compromised, provided you have access to a majority, you will be able to access your wallet and safely migrate your funds.

  • When choosing the password your share files will be encrypted with, please note it down or choose one you can remember. It cannot be changed or recovered.

  • Use a strong password for your account (minimum 15 characters, both upper and lower case letters, numbers, symbols and does not contain your name or the name of the service you are using).
